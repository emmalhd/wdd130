History

Adventure Rapids Co was founded in 2024 by Emma Hardy. Growing up Emma moved around a lot and lived near many major and famous rivers. She never lost her passion for white water rafting and decided to share that the love she has for the activity with other people. Adventure Rapids is looking forward to bringing joy and adventure to our community!
